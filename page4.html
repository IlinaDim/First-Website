♆ Favorite Book ♆

I first read The Lightning Thief in 5th grade and I immediately fell in love with the series. It will always have a special place in my heart because it was the first novel that had no trouble fully captivating me with its world building and characters. As a child, this was the first book that I found had an actually compelling main character with a view of the world that I actually related to; Percy Jackson as a character was written to represent all the children who had trouble fitting in due to things out of their control and no matter how much they tried to change themselves for others, yet eventually found their place by meeting people who accept him despite his faults and that sentiment always stuck with me, even from such a young age.
